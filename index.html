My Passions

Riding

I have loved bicycles and riding them since I got my first bike. There was a time when I rode for five hours or more. Slowly, riding became a part of my life, so much so that I started collaborating with a food delivery company that works particularly with riders, with the main objective of creating a better environment. I'm still working with them, and sometimes I do food deliveries purely for the pleasure of riding, not just for money.

Photography

In school, I was fascinated by the colors and behavior of light and shadow. That was one of the reasons why, after finishing high school, I started studying photography at Sapientia University in Cluj-Napoca. During that time, I honed my skills in event photography.

Economy

In high school, we had economics lessons that piqued my interest in understanding how the mechanism works. During those months, I started putting away money and managing it with more discipline. Some years later, I also tried trading on the forex market. My interest in the economy was another reason why I started my subcontract in 2019. Initially, I started with delivery activities, but after completing audio training, I added two more activities: studio sound recording and live audio mixing. I plan to further expand my subcontract in the future.

Web Portfolio

RiderBox webpage

The RiderBox webpage's role is to facilitate the creation of a technical dossier for theatre performances. It should contain sketches of the scenery, lighting, and audio, as well as all the information about a performance. It will be a personalized webpage with a login request. When you register a new account, you can add your content to it. The 'Home' page displays the list of saved performances, which can also be accessed through the 'Performances' dropdown button in the header. It is designed to be used by a single account, and at the 'Add new' option, you can provide the performance's information separately.

Currently, it is not available on GitHub.

About me

Hi, I'm Ferencz!

According to 16personalities.com, I am an advocate, an INTJ-A/INTJ-F person. I have a great ability to control stressful situations and locate and resolve their causes. I am open to new challenges and opportunities, and it's never too late for me to learn something new and develop all of my skills.
I like to write accurate schedules for the future and stick to them. Time is my best friend, and in most cases, I arrive at a place early or on time. The same is true for finishing a project.
I was born in 1995 in Cluj-Napoca and spent most of my childhood in a village. I finished a theoretical high school in Gherla with a focus on philology. In the summer of 2014, I decided to enroll in Sapientia University, specializing in Cinematography, Photography, and Media. I finished my studies without a license exam in 2017. During this time, I also started working with the Puck Puppet Theatre as a sound designer and later as a stage technician. Between 2018 and 2019, I completed an adult course for Sound Engineering at Sapientia University.
In 2020, during the lockdown, I decided to move away from my family. One of the main reasons was to avoid infecting myself and my family with the virus, and the second reason was to find a place where I could expand my abilities and try different lifestyles. In the summer of 2019, I started a subcontract with a food delivery company, and a year later, I added two other activities to the subcontract. Starting in September 2021, I had a contract with a restaurant where my job was delivering their goods.
Besides learning and working, I have had many hobbies, including ceramics, photography, cycling, cooking, and coding. At one time, I also played guitar. In my leisure time, I prefer to watch movies, read books, and plan my next day, week, or year with at least 95% completion.
I have been interested in coding and web development since high school, so in the summer of 2020, I signed up for coding courses on platformait.ro, now teach.bit, an online platform where I learned some new things in writing HTML, CSS2, and some JS lines. I am committed to continuing to learn in this area.
